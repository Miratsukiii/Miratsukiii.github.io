Rottberg is confusing.

Rottberg's plot focuses on a young man named Richard Arvids, devastated still by the disappearance of his father nearly 20 years ago, he searches for
answers in the a very unlikely place, the video game that his father was developing for the playstation in 1998. This investigation would later lead
to the strangest and most stressful years of his life.

Rottberg's development has been rather all-over the place. It started in 2020 as a parody of Petscop ala Sheriff Domestic, and later
got rebooted into a slightly more distinguished project.
After finishing Sheriff Domestic in Real, I returned to the drawing board and began Rottberg again, this time with a much
higher scope and production quality, bringing in the talents of my closest friends, and my partner to create something truly special.

Unfortunately, Rottberg is nowhere near finished right now, so I am unable to show much, you are however able to view an archive of every
early iteration of it, to give you an idea of what you're getting into.
