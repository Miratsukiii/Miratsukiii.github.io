Sheriff Domestic in Real

Sheriff Domestic in Real was my unofficial spinoff to Sheriff Domestic, it started off as a simple parody of Sheriff Domestic
and gradually evolved (mutated?) into an a dadaist art project/ARG. The latter half of the series leading up to the end acted as a testbed
for ideas and concepts I wanted to use in later projects.


I made this one after a few friends and I binged the entire Eric Andre show.

I think towards the end I wanted to experiment with telling a story via seemingly unrelated absurd shitposty videos
I don't think I did that bad, people seemed to generally be pretty invested by the end.
The series started March 13th of 2020, and ended exactly 3 years later in 2023.
