Household Felony

Household Felony was one of the first and probably most major of all the projects I have worked on,
it initially started life as nothing more than just a comedic parody of Petscop, in similar nature to Sheriff Domestic, but then it rapidly evolved into a pseudo spiritual successor of sorts,
telling an unrelated story with concepts and ideas established in Petscop already.


I personally believe episode 11 was the best of them all, so I like introducing people to the series with it.

Despite my history of denouncing any mention of Household Felony, I've sort of come to my senses as of late, and began appreciating
what it did for me, and just that it's not nearly as bad as I thought it was, it occupies a permenant space in my head, even long after cancelling it.
